Smothered Cheese Omelet

This omelet is quick, easy, versatile, and delicious! It can be made with a variety of pickled pepper juices, ranging from very hot to mild, or with white vinegar for a non-spicy twist.

  • Serves: 1
  • Prep time: 2 mins
  • Cook time: 10 mins

Ingredients

Omelet:
  1. 2 eggs
  2. 1 tbsp heavy cream
  3. 1/2 cup (minus 2 tbsp) mozzarella cheese
  4. 1 tbsp butter
  5. Salt and pepper to taste
Sauce:
  1. 2 fluid ounces heavy cream
  2. 2 tbsp mozzarella cheese
  3. 1-2 tbsp pickled pepper juice or white vinegar

Instructions

Omelet:
  1. Combine 2 large eggs and 1 tbsp heavy cream in a bowl and whisk wiht a fork. Set aside.
  2. Heat 1 tbsp butter in a small frying pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour egg mixture into frying pan once butter is melted and pan is heated.
  4. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Cook until eggs are no longer jiggly, then flip.
  6. Turn off heat and add mozzarella cheese.
  7. Plate omelet and set aside.
Sauce:
  1. Heat 2 fluid ounces of heavy cream over medium heat in a small sauce pan.
  2. Once heated, whisk in 2 tbsp mozzarella cheese. Whisk until well combined and creamy.
  3. Add 1-2 tbsp pepper juice or vinegar and continue whisking until well combined and creamy.
  4. Pour sauce over omelet and enjoy.

Substitutions/Additions

  • A great alternative to mozzarella cheese is oaxacan cheese, pepperjack cheese, or Monterey jack cheese.
  • The omelet can be filled with other ingredients such as diced chile pepers, sauteed mushrooms, chorizo, etc.
  • Sliced avocado or diced chives make a great topping to this simple dish.
