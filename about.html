Autumn All Year is a site dedicated to recipes that suit a mostly animal-based diet, which includes little to no plants.

I am Autumn and I created this page. As a hobbyist photographer and foodie, I love to come up with new and invetive ways to prepare foods that adhere to my own dietary restrictions.

Combining my passions for web development, cooking, and photography I created this website so that I could share my recipes with others.

The recipes are relatively simple, with few ingredients and limited steps. All recipes feature whole, real foods and can be prepared with common household cooking utensils and appliances. The instructions are intended to be easy to follow and a list of substitutions/additions is included with each recipe.
