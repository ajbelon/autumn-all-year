Crustless Quiche

These delicious crustless quiches were very easy to make and are really tasty with a light, airy texture and crusty, flaky sides. They are a delicious meal to have as either breakfast or dinner.

  • Serves: 2
  • Prep time: 15 mins
  • Cook time: 40 mins

Ingredients

  1. 6 large eggs
  2. 3/4 cup heavy cream
  3. 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  4. 1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese
  5. Fresh lemon thyme
  6. Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. Blend the first three ingredients in a blender or in a bowl with an immersion blender.
  3. Add the remaining ingredients and stir to combine.
  4. Pour the mixture into glass baking dishes or muffin tins, leaving about 2 inches at the top for expansion.
  5. Bake for approximately 40 mins. This is done when the top is slightly browned and the egg mixture is set (ie no jiggle). Keep an eye on these while baking. Baking time will vary based on the size of your dish and based upon the variance of oven temps.
