Mustard Cream Sauce

This sauce is extremely simple, with just two ingredients. It can be made with many different types of mustards, making a versatile accompaniment to many dishes. It's creamy with a nice mustard tang!

  • Serves: 2
  • Prep time: 1 mins
  • Cook time: 5 mins

Ingredients

  1. 2 fluid ounces heavy cream
  2. 2 tbsp mustard of your liking

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a small sauce pan. Whisk to combine and heat over medium heat until heated through.

Substitutions/Additions

  • For a thicker sauce (or if you just don't have heavy cream), mix in 1/2 cup of sour cream instead of the heavy cream.
